I've seen a big spike in Halo downloads, but the downloads for hc-php, Skittle and Substrate have hardly moved. While I am very excited that people are wanting to try out Halo, it concerns me that nobody is downloading the dependencies. :) So I have added the dependency notices to the Halo website (it was only on the Google Code page previously) in an effort to hopefully spread the word.

It also occurs to me that with the lack of documentation people downloading Halo for the first time are likely going to be very confused and probably rm -rf the package pretty quickly. I am going to set a goal for myself to get a sample Halo project online with some basic documentation to get people started using Halo.

While you are waiting for Halo documentation, I suggest taking a look at Substrate to get acquainted with it. Since Halo is built on Substrate, a working knowledge of Substrate is going to be critical. Unlike Halo, Substrate actually has at least a bit of documentation to get you started.

Also, if you take the time to download Halo, please take a few extra seconds to join the Halo General Discussion group and at the very least introduce yourself. I like to ramble on at great length, so if you have any questions, please feel free to post away! That is what the group is for!
