The first snapshots of Safsi are available for download. To be honest, this realease is not super interesting. It simply contains a Local Disk implementation of safsi_IFileSystem, safsi_IContainer and safsi_IObject that is mostly completed and mostly unit tested.

The coverage of the unit testing isn't super great and safsi_localdisk_Object.writeDataToStream() and  and safsi_localdisk_Container.deleteObject() are not yet implemented.

I'd love some feedback on the idea for this project and the direction I've gone with it.
