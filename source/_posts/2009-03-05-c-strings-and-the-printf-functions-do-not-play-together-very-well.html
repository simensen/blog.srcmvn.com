Learned a new lesson today in C++. Although many functions and methods apparently support the char* to string conversions pretty easily, the *printf family of functions do not. I suppose this makes sense now that I know this, but it really did not make a lot of sense for the better part of two days. Thanks to the stackoverflow community for helping me realize that the problem I've been wrestling with actually had nothing at all to do with getopts_long at all.
